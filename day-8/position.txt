Positions

1. position:static; (default)
Every element respects another element, it does not overlap

2. position:relative;
Arranges wrt its original position
Offset : top,bottom,left,right
Content can overlap

3. position:fixed;
Arranges wrt the viewport (display screen)
Ek hi jagah fix rhega
Offset: top,bottom,left,right
overlap can happen

4. position: absolute;
arranges wrt parent element and parent should be relative 
(if parent not present then arranges wrt viewport)

5. position:sticky 
works only in presence of scroll
